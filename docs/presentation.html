The Problem

Marketing agencies face significant challenges that hinder scalability and efficiency:

  • Time-consuming discovery calls averaging 2-3 hours per client.
  • Inconsistent information gathering across team members.
  • Manual content creation requiring multiple iterations.
  • Delayed campaign launches due to back-and-forth communications.
"We're spending more time in meetings than creating campaigns." - Client's initial complaint

The Solution

I developed an AI-powered interview system that transforms a 3-hour manual process into a 20-minute automated interview by:

  • Conducting intelligent, adaptive client interviews.
  • Automatically generating platform-specific marketing content.
  • Maintaining brand consistency across all materials.
  • Scaling effortlessly with client growth.
